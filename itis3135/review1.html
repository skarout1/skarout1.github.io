My Remarks

  • Design:
    • I really like the design and layout of the webpage. It is really simple and elegant
    • The contrast between the color choices is good
    • The Green color is easy on the eyes
    • The font is uniform and again a good professional looking choice
  • Layout:
    • The layout of the page is also simple and easy to navigate
    • There is are clear header and main elements to help with this
    • There however is not a clear footer. There is an "all rights reserved" at the bottum, but it just sits there. Not seperate from the rest of the page at all
    • There are clear and easy to find links in the header leading to the different pages on the site
    • The company name is cleary posted
  • Content:
    • The content of the page leaves a lot to be desired
    • There is very little to no genuine contetn on the actual page to tell the visitor of the site anything about the client
    • The golfing photos throughout the page are a nice touch
    • There is not much else to say because of the lack of content
  • Interactive Element:
    • There is a small link to google maps on the contact map that shows the client office. I think this is a nice touch and interactive for the user of the webpage
  • Requirements:
    • The page does meet most of the requirments for the client project, but does not go above and beyond
